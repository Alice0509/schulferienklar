Practical travel guidance, not opening-hour listings

Germany Travel Checker should not maintain exact opening hours for individual shops, supermarkets, cafés, bakeries, tobacco shops, pharmacies or vending machines. Those details change too often.

Instead, the product should explain stable travel patterns: regular supermarkets and many shops are usually closed on Sundays and public holidays, while major train stations, airports, gas stations, kiosks, cafés, bakeries, restaurants, hotels and vending machines may be useful fallback options.

Public holidays Some holidays are nationwide, while others only apply in certain federal states.
Sunday closures Regular shops and supermarkets are usually closed on Sundays.
Travel crowds School holiday periods may affect trains, hotels, attractions and prices.
