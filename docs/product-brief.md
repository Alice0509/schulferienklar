# Product Brief

## Name

Schulferienklar

## Domain

schulferienklar.de

## Tagline

Der klare Schulferienkalender für Deutschland.

## Product definition

Schulferienklar helps people understand German school holidays by Bundesland and use them for planning.

## Primary users

- People living in Germany
- Parents and families
- People planning holidays, work schedules, travel, accommodation or childcare
- International residents who need a clearer explanation of German school holiday rhythms

## Core value

- Clean interface
- Official sources
- Calendar-friendly data
- No distracting advertising in the MVP
- Clear disclaimer about school-specific dates

## Language strategy

- Primary: German
- Secondary: English
- Optional later: Korean and other languages
