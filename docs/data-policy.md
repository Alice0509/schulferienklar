# Data Policy

Schulferienklar includes only official state-level school holiday data.

## Include

An event can be included when:

1. It is published by KMK or an official Bundesland education authority.
2. It has a concrete start date and end date.
3. It applies at Bundesland level or is listed in the official state holiday regulation.
4. It is relevant to school holiday planning.

## Exclude

The default dataset excludes:

- bewegliche Ferientage
- pädagogische Tage
- individual school closure days
- Gymnasium-specific calendar dates
- Realschule-specific calendar dates
- Grundschule-specific calendar dates
- public holidays in the MVP
- user-specific personal events

## UI disclaimer

DE:
Schulferienklar zeigt die offiziellen Schulferien der Bundesländer. Bewegliche Ferientage, pädagogische Tage und schulspezifische Termine können je nach Schule abweichen und sind nicht enthalten.

EN:
Schulferienklar shows the official school holidays of the German federal states. Movable holidays, pedagogical days and school-specific dates may vary by school and are not included.
